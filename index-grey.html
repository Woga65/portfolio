About me

I´m a Front-End Developer, a Model Avionics Enthusiast and a teacher of dancing who loves pushing boundaries.

MY JOURNEY

began with my first own computer, an Epson HX-20. While I still went to school, I helped in a local IT company with projects for which they had no trained staff. Later I worked six years for a newspaper publisher where I managed a team of software developers.

I FELL IN LOVE

again when I built a website for my dance studio. Realizing how badly I missed programming, I began to learn embedded C and started writing firmware for model aircraft.

I WAS LOOKING

for an effective way to enhance my professional skills and opted for a project-based method of learning. I relied on a team of experienced web developers who helped me broaden my knowledge and make great advances.

MY GOALS

are to prove my experience and know-how in practice, and to deepen my knowledge. To meet future challenges, I am practicing backend skills with Developer Academy.
